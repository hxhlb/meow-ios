Build 2026060702 (1.3.1)

WHAT'S NEW
• Hotfix for a "connected but no traffic" stall introduced in build
  2026060701: heavy sleep/wake cycling could pile up leaked internal
  timers until the engine stopped responding entirely (no traffic, no
  stats, toggle required). The leak, the lock behavior that amplified
  it, and a restart race it exposed are all fixed.
• New engine watchdog: if the engine ever stops responding for ~30
  seconds, the tunnel now restarts itself automatically (brief visible
  reconnect) instead of staying silently dead until you toggle it.
• Everything from 2026060701: long-idle fix, IPv6↔IPv4 transition
  handling, UDP address-family fallback, control-API hardening.

FOR TESTERS
• Sleep/wake stress: use the phone normally with frequent lock/unlock
  for a few hours; traffic should never stall. If you ever see a brief
  disconnect+reconnect, that's the new watchdog doing its job — please
  report it (it means something still wedged underneath).
• Overnight idle: leave the VPN connected overnight; traffic should
  flow immediately in the morning.
• IPv6 flip: on dual-stack Wi-Fi, disable IPv6 on the router; browsing
  should recover within seconds.
• Issues: https://github.com/madeye/meow-ios/issues
